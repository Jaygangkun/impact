IMPACT is a grassroots community development plan with a focus on economic prosperity, placemaking, healthy living and wellbeing, and educational excellence rooted in the core belief that every resident has the right to strive to create the environment they desire. Collaboration drives the creation of the plan and implementation of its objectives.

Healthy Living

AND WELL-BEING

VISION: Become the healthiest county in Arkansas, committed to keeping residents active and physically and emotionally well.

PHYSICAL ACTIVITY: Provide a variety of opportunities to get all residents outdoors and physically active.

SOCIAL WELLNESS: Reimagine opportunities for providing socially active lifestyles for residents at every stage of life.

KNOWLEDGE OF AND ACCESS TO HEALTH AND WELLNESS OPPORTUNITIES: Increase knowledge of and access to health and wellness opportunities and social events.

INSPIRE HEALTHIER CHOICES: Create educational and other opportunities that inspire residents to make healthier choices.

SOCIAL COMPETENCE & EMOTIONAL MATURITY: Increase the number of children that enter kindergarten socially on-track and ready to learn by prioritizing social-emotional needs and learning (SEL) curriculum and intentional teaching of prosocial and helping behaviors.

PHYSICAL CHILD DEVELOPMENT: Increase the number of children that enter kindergarten physically on-track and ready to learn by expanding access to screenings and providing more opportunities for families to build gross and fine motor skills.

ORAL HEALTH: Increase the proportion of low-income children who receive preventive dental services by expanding oral health knowledge among early educators and families, connecting families to local dentists, and easing barriers to accessing dental care.

SUBSTANCE USE: Increase the number of community programs and projects that focus on substance use research, prevention, and treatment.

MOBILITY: Increase support for the expanded development of the greenway trail system and proactively support the development of public transportation to increase community access.

IMAGE: Improve the appearance and image of Independence County.

REGIONAL MEDICAL HUB: Support the growth of regional healthcare organizations.
