IMPACT is a grassroots community development plan with a focus on economic prosperity, placemaking, healthy living and wellbeing, and educational excellence rooted in the core belief that every resident has the right to strive to create the environment they desire. Collaboration drives the creation of the plan and implementation of its objectives.
